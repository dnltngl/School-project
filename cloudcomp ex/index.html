ABOUT

Holy Angel University is a private Catholic university located in Angeles City, Pampanga, Philippines. The university has a rich history dating back to 1933 when it was founded by the Holy Rosary Sisters.

University days at Holy Angel University are a time of exploration, growth, and learning. Students are provided with a wide range of academic programs, extracurricular activities, and support services to help them achieve their goals and develop into well-rounded individuals.

During their university days, students are exposed to a diverse community of learners, faculty, and staff, which allows them to develop their critical thinking skills, cultural awareness, and social responsibility. They are encouraged to participate in various student organizations, clubs, and sports teams, which provide opportunities for them to engage in teamwork, leadership, and community service.
